About Us

Upkar Microfinance is dedicated to providing financial support and empowering individuals to achieve their goals. We aim to help small businesses, farmers, and individuals by offering easy and accessible loan services. Our mission is to promote financial growth, self-reliance, and economic development in rural and urban areas. We believe in trust, transparency, and customer satisfaction, ensuring that every individual gets the opportunity to grow. With a strong commitment to service, we are working continuously to make financial services simple, fast, and reliable for everyone. THANK YOU...
